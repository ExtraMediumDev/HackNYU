Welcome to Driver De-Stress

Take a Moment to Relax

Great job pulling over! Here are a few quick tips to reduce stress:

  • Close your eyes and take three slow, deep breaths.
  • Roll your shoulders backward and forward a few times to loosen tension.
  • Listen to a calm audio track or simple nature sounds if it helps.

Stay safe and remember: a brief break can go a long way to keep you relaxed on the road.
